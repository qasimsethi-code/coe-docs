## Project Categories

The projects above can be grouped into several strategic categories that reflect Qasim's holistic approach to transforming the COE's pricing operations.

**Core Valuation & Workflow** projects form the backbone of the pricing operation. The Vehicle Valuation Workflow, Dual-Agent Architecture, and Appraisal Engine Bot work together to deliver mathematically justified, explainable valuations with full audit trails.

**Automation & Integration** projects focus on eliminating manual work and connecting disparate systems. The Autorola PAD Automation, SAP P01 Integration, and COE CLI fall into this category, each addressing a specific integration gap in the current workflow.

**Tools & Analytics** projects provide the analytical foundation for pricing decisions. The AFM Pricing Engine, af-pricing-agent, Market Intelligence assets, and DSR Consolidation scripts ensure that every valuation is grounded in current market data.

**Governance & Standards** projects ensure that all outputs meet enterprise compliance requirements. The GCC Appraisal Template, Appraisal Quality & Evidence standards, and PnP.PowerShell installation all contribute to a governance-first operating model.
