## Promotion Case

Qasim's promotion case is built on a foundation of demonstrated technical leadership, measurable business impact, and consistent delivery of enterprise-grade solutions. The case is supported by several categories of evidence.

### Technical Leadership Evidence

Qasim has designed and built the COE's most complex automation systems, including the Vehicle Valuation Workflow, Dual-Agent Architecture, and Autorola PAD Automation. These projects demonstrate an ability to architect enterprise-grade solutions that balance innovation with compliance.

### Business Impact Evidence

The automations and tools built by Qasim have measurably improved the COE's operational efficiency. The semi-automated Instant Auction workflow, DSR consolidation script, and standardized appraisal templates have reduced manual effort, improved consistency, and accelerated decision-making.

### Innovation Evidence

The Dual-Agent Architecture (Claude + Copilot) represents a novel approach to automotive pricing that has been validated end-to-end. The af-pricing-agent PWA demonstrates the ability to rapidly prototype and deploy user-facing tools. The creative PnP.PowerShell installation via GitHub API chunk reassembly showcases problem-solving under constraints.

### Governance & Compliance Evidence

Every system Qasim builds includes audit trails, explainability layers, and rollback capabilities. The zero-hallucination mandate for the Appraisal Engine Bot and the compliance-first design of all Power Platform solutions demonstrate a mature understanding of enterprise governance requirements.
