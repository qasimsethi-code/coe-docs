## Executive Summary

Qasim Sethi is a Pricing Appraiser in the Automotive / Used Cars Center of Excellence (COE) at Al-Futtaim Group in Dubai. He is the primary architect and owner of a compliance-grade automotive valuation platform that combines pricing intelligence, workflow automation, and AI assistance into a unified, enterprise-ready system.

Operating with an architect's mindset, Qasim balances the need for rapid automation with strict enterprise compliance. His work demonstrates a clear trajectory toward leadership and promotion within the organization, underpinned by deep domain expertise in used vehicle appraisal, wholesale and retail valuation, and governance-aware automotive workflow automation.

---

## What He's Building

Qasim is focused on building and refining an **end-to-end digitized used-car appraisal, pricing, and governance system**. His flagship project, the **Vehicle Valuation Workflow**, integrates VIN decoding, market intelligence, and penalty logic to provide mathematically justified and explainable vehicle valuations.

To achieve this, he leverages a sophisticated **Dual-Agent Architecture** (Claude + Microsoft Copilot) and extensive Power Platform orchestration spanning SharePoint, Power Apps, Power Automate, and Copilot Studio. He is also developing automated workflows for Autorola queue management and SAP P01 data integration, alongside various analytical tools and scripts.
